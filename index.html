How to Use

1. Each turn, you'll see a throw (e.g., T18 T17 T2).

2. Calculate the total score for the throw and enter it in "Total Score".

3. Subtract the total from your current score (start at 501) and enter the remainder in "Remaining Score".

4. Press "Add Score" to continue. To win, you must reach exactly 0 with a double.

5. Use "Restart" to start over.
